Board dimensions: Cut perfboard to 13 × 10 holes (~3.3 × 2.5 cm). BNO085 breakout overhangs right edge — only pin header sits on the board. Secure overhang with hot glue or epoxy.
Routing: 3V3→VIN is a short top-side L-shape (3 hops). GND→GND runs on the bottom side via gap col J (4 hops). I2C crossing: BNO085 pin order is SCL-then-SDA (opposite XIAO), so SDA routes on the top side as L-shape (B5→J5→J6→K6) and SCL routes on the bottom side as mirror L-shape (B6→J6→J5→K5). They cross at col J on opposite sides — no short. Max wire: ~25.4 mm (10 holes).
Decoupling: 100 nF cap bridges VIN and GND right next to BNO085.
Battery: BAT+ → external switch → LiPo(+). BAT− → LiPo(−) direct. Switch mounted on housing. Charging via TP4057 module (separate station — disconnect LiPo from board first).
